      How to Use MBLD Scramble Finder

      1. Set the BLD and scrambling orientations relative to WCA default, then save.
      2. Click stickers on the cube canvas to match the pattern you’re searching for.
      3. Enter scrambles through the “Enter Scrambles” button and paste one per line.
      4. Matching scrambles appear in the table; click one to preview it on the cube.
      5. Use the color selector (bottom-right square) to switch the drawing color.
